# RAMMS Volleyball Rules: League-Specific Rules

The Junior League covers grades 3-5 and the Senior League covers grades 6-8. Nearly all rules are shared; the following items differ by league.

**1. Serving Lines and Wristbands:**
- Each league may begin the season with multiple forward serving lines. Coaches designate where each player serves from before the match. A server must wear a wristband matching their serving line and serve from that line for the entire match. Any player without a wristband serves from regulation distance.
- **Junior League:** To start the season, 3 servers serve from the 10-foot line, 3 from 15 feet behind the net, and 2 from 20 feet from the net. Each week, 1 wristband is removed until each team has only 2 servers from 15 feet and all others from regulation distance.
- **Senior League:** To start the season, 1 player may serve from 25 feet and everyone else from regulation. After week one, all Senior players serve from regulation.
- Any overhand server in either league must serve from behind the back line.

**2. Game Ball:**
- Subject to the Commissioner's discretion, the **Senior League** uses a Tachikara SV-18S game ball, and the **Junior League** uses a Tachikara SV-MNC Volley-Lite game ball.

**3. Substitutes Across Leagues:**
- A Junior division player may play as a substitute in the Senior division (a substitute must be in the same or lower grade as the player being replaced).
