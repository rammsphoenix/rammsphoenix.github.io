# RAMMS Flag Football Rules: Division-Specific Rules

**1. Kickoff Spot:**
- **Freshman:** Kicks off from midfield.
- **Sophomore:** Kicks off from 5 yards behind midfield.
- **Junior:** Kicks off from its own 10-yard line.
- **Senior:** Kicks off from its own 5-yard line.

**2. Quarterback Pass Clock:**
- **Freshman:** 7-second pass clock.
- **Sophomore:** 7-second pass clock.
- **Junior:** 5-second pass clock.
- **Senior:** 5-second pass clock.
- *In all divisions, if the Quarterback has not thrown within the pass-clock time, the play is dead at the spot the Q.B. last possessed the ball; the rule no longer applies once the ball is handed off.*

**3. Coach on Field During Offense:**
- **Freshman:** One coach allowed, approximately 5 yards behind the deepest set offensive player; may not interfere.
- **Sophomore:** One coach allowed, approximately 5 yards behind the deepest set offensive player; may not interfere.
- **Junior:** One coach allowed on the field; may not interfere.
- **Senior:** No coach allowed on the field for offense.

**4. Coach on Field During Defense:**
- **Freshman:** One coach allowed, but must be a minimum of 15 yards past the line of scrimmage (after one warning, violations are a 5-yard automatic first down).
- **Sophomore:** One coach allowed, but must be a minimum of 15 yards past the line of scrimmage (after one warning, violations are a 5-yard automatic first down).
- **Junior:** No coach allowed on the field for defense.
- **Senior:** No coach allowed on the field for defense.

**5. Football Size:**
- **Freshman:** "Pee Wee" football.
- **Sophomore:** "Pee Wee" football.
- **Junior:** "Junior" football.
- **Senior:** "Youth" football.
- *Note: The source rules state the "Varsity Division" shall use a Youth football; "Varsity" is treated here as the same oldest division as "Senior."*

**6. Call-Ups Between Divisions:**
- **Freshman:** Not eligible to call up substitute players (no lower division).
- **Sophomore:** May fill its roster with substitute players from the Freshman Division for regular season games.
- **Junior:** May fill its roster with substitute players from the Sophomore Division for regular season games.
- **Senior:** May fill its roster with substitute players from the Junior Division for regular season games.
- *In all divisions, the Director of Football Operations must approve substitutes, and a substitute may be called up by a team only once per season.*
