# RAMMS Basketball Rules: Division-Specific Rules

**1. Hoop Height, Ball Size, and Free Throw Distance:**
- **Boys K & Girls K-1st:** 8-foot hoop, 25.5" ball, ~10-foot free throw line [18-20].
- **Boys 1st-2nd & Girls 2nd-3rd:** 8-foot hoop, 27.5" ball, ~13-foot free throw line [18, 21, 22].
- **Boys 3rd-4th & Girls 4th-5th:** 9-foot hoop, 28.5" ball, ~13-foot free throw line [18, 23, 24].
- **Boys 5th-8th & Girls 6th-8th:** 10-foot hoop, 29.5" ball (Boys) / 28.5" ball (Girls), standard 15-foot free throw line [18, 25, 26].

**2. Lane Violations:**
- **Boys K, Girls K-1st, & Girls 2nd-3rd:** No lane violation is enforced [19, 20, 22, 27].
- **Boys 1st-2nd, Boys 3rd-4th, & Girls 4th-5th:** 5 seconds in the key [21, 23, 24, 27].
- **Boys 5th-8th & Girls 6th-8th:** 3 seconds in the key [25-27].

**3. Points Awarded for Shooting Fouls (Outside Last 2 Minutes):**
- **Boys K, 1st-2nd, Girls K-1st, 2nd-3rd, 4th-5th:** 1 point plus the ball for all shots (Note: Girls 4th-5th rules have some ambiguity regarding 3-point fouls) [28].
- **Boys 3rd-4th, 5th-8th, Girls 6th-8th:** 1 point plus the ball for 2-point shots; 2 points plus the ball for 3-point shots [28].

**4. Defensive Limitations:**
- **Kindergarten / Girls K-1st:** Strict man-to-man defense only [19, 20]. Defenders may not steal or swipe at the ball [19, 20, 29]. Double teaming is prohibited everywhere [19, 20, 29]. Switching is allowed on picks but players must switch back at the first opportunity [19, 20, 29]. Defenders can use a simple sag (no more than 3 feet if the offensive player is inside the arc) [19, 20, 30].
- **Boys 1st-2nd:** Man-to-man defense [21]. Steals from their own man and swiping are allowed [21, 29]. Double teaming is allowed only in the paint [21, 29]. Simple sag rules apply [21, 30, 31].
- **Boys 3rd-4th, Girls 2nd-3rd, Girls 4th-5th:** Man-to-man defense [22-24]. Steals and swipes are allowed [22-24, 29]. Double teaming is permitted only in the paint [22-24, 29]. These divisions introduce the weak-side sag restriction: weak-side defenders cannot sag into the paint unless their man is within 3 feet of the paint [22-24, 31].
- **Boys 5th-8th & Girls 6th-8th:** Teams may play three-quarter court man-to-man defense, as well as zone defense and double teaming inside the top-of-key extended in the front court [25, 26, 29, 32]. Steals, swipes, and blocks on anyone are permitted anywhere legal defense is played [29].

**5. Backcourt Defense Areas:**
- **All Younger Divisions (Through Boys 4th/Girls 5th):** No defense past half court during regular play [17]. In the last two minutes/overtime, full court man-to-man is allowed (double-teaming permitted except in K/1st grade divisions) [17].
- **Older Divisions (Boys 5th-8th/Girls 6th-8th):** No defense past the top-of-key extended line in the backcourt during regular play [17, 30]. Full court man-to-man with double-teaming is allowed in the last two minutes/overtime [17].

**6. Wristband Matchup System:**
- **Boys K, Boys 1st-2nd, Girls K-1st, & Girls 2nd-3rd:** Players are required to wear colored wristbands to visually enforce man-to-man matchups [18, 33-37]. Colors match players by skill level (strongest to weakest) to avoid mismatches [33-36]. Players may only guard an opponent with the same colored wristband, except to stop a fast break or when switching on a pick and roll [33-37].
- **Older Divisions:** No wristbands are used [18].
